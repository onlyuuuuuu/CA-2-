Welcome to the Hangman Game!

Hangman is a classic word-guessing game. Here are the rules:

  • You will have 2 minutes to guess the word.
  • For each correct letter guessed, you earn points.
  • For each incorrect guess, you lose points.
  • Your goal is to guess the word correctly before time runs out.
  • Score
  • 0 chances - 100 points
  • 1 chance - 80 points
  • 2 chances - 60 points
  • 3 chance - 40 points
  • 4 chances - 20 points
  • 5 chance - 10 points
  • 6 chances - 0 points
